What if we bought price protection?

A "collar" pairs a floor (a minimum price you can't fall below) with a ceiling (a maximum you can't rise above) — the ceiling pays for the floor, so it costs nothing up front. This page walks through what one would cover on next harvest, so you can decide whether it's worth a call to Ritchie.
